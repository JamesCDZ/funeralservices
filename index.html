Welcome to the website of David Armstrong - serving the families and communities of South Yorkshire since 1990

Find out more
Created by David in 1998 Funeral Management Services was the forum from which bespoke and personalised funeral services could be offered to the people of South Yorkshire who had very little by way of options at that time. Pioneering alternative funerals has been a lifelong dedicated passion and the key to helping the communities of South Yorkshire to say a meaningful and relevant farewell to loved ones.It has been a privilege and pleasure to have been been able to help families and friends at a difficult time.

Our experience over 30 years we have facilitated many funeral requirements including themed funerals, horse-drawn, motorcycle ride outs bands, bagpipes and personalised ceremonies enabling the funeral to become a positive and memorable occasion even though it is sad.
